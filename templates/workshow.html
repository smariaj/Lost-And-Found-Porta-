How It Works

Follow these simple steps to reunite lost items with their owners

1️⃣ Found or Lost an Item

Start by identifying whether you have lost something or found someone’s belongings.

2️⃣ Snap Its Picture

Take a clear picture of the item. This helps in matching and identifying it easily.

3️⃣ Check for Matches

If you’ve lost something, our system will help you find possible matches from recently found items.

4️⃣ Go to the Watchman Desk

If your lost item is available, visit the watchman’s desk where found items are safely kept.

5️⃣ Provide Proof & Claim

Show valid proof of ownership (like a photo, ID, or description) and claim your item securely.
